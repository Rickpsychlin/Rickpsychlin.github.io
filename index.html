Department of Linguistics, University of Florida

Welcome! I’m a fresh PhD graduate in psycholinguistics from Deparment of Linguistics, University of Florida. My research interests include sentence processing, cognitive control and language processing, corpus linguistics and usage-based approach to language learning. My dissertation project investigates factors that may contribute to the processing of a temporarily ambiguous Mandarin Chinese construction VN1deN2.
